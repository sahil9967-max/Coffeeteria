Our Story

Founded in 2020, Coffeeteria began as a small passion project between friends who shared a love for exceptional coffee. What started as a humble corner shop has grown into a beloved community gathering place.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring every cup supports both quality and ethical practices. Our master baristas are trained in the art of coffee making, bringing you the perfect balance of flavor and craftsmanship.
